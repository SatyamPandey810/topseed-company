3 Steps on how to get started

01

Register

Create an account through our signup page or mobile app by fill-in your first name, last name, email address and password. A confirmation mail will be sent to your email, verify it and procceed with the next step.

02

Verify Identity

This is the KYC stage also known as know-your-customers, were we would request several information about the individual or business for security reasons. This information include a valid ID card, proof of address or any verifiable legal documents of business.

03

Start Transaction

After the KYC process is completed and your account has been approved, you can now start using our platform officially. Now you will recognized as an official client of our platform, which means you get to send & receive money accross all countries that we support, pay your utility bills, internet subscription and a lot more.
